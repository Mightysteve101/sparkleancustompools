About Our Company

Sparklean Custom Pools is the top choice for El Paso pool builders. We are a family owned and operated business servicing El Paso and surrounding areas. We pride ourselves in several areas including: quality of pool build, efficiency of crews, great communication, and nothing less than 100% customer satisfaction. With decades of construction experience, our team strives to ensure you are getting the backyard of your dreams at the most competitive and affordable prices.

Gabriel, owners and operators, are directly involved with all facets of Sparklean Custom Pools, giving the customer constant interaction with the people who have built our business from the ground up! This gives our customers the satisfaction and comfort of knowing that we are going to take care of you and be there every step of the way to make the pool building process a seamless one. We look forward to meeting you and helping you along the way to create the backyard you’ve been waiting for. Looking for a pool builder in the Fort Worth area? Give us a call!
