Our Services

We Construct Exterior Aquatic Spaces Tailored To Your Lifestyle.

Sparklean Custom Pools is here to help you for the life of your pool. We are a full service pool company whether you want a new custom pool or to remodel an older one. We also provide pool maintenance services. We are available by phone for any question big or small.

  • 01

    Planning begins with our collaborative design process where we capture your vision and transform it into detailed blueprints that consider your lifestyle, property features, and aesthetic preferences.

  • 02

    Digging commences with precision excavation using state-of-the-art equipment to carefully shape your pool's foundation, ensuring proper depth, dimensions, and preparation for the structural elements.

  • 03

    Concrete application involves expert craftsmanship as our team meticulously forms and finishes premium structural materials to create a durable, long-lasting foundation that will support your pool for decades.

  • 04

    Plastering represents the artisanal final touch as we apply luxurious interior finishes that create a smooth, beautiful surface while ensuring water integrity and complementing your design with vibrant, lasting quality.
